🪷Maharashtra Tourism🪷

Maharashtra (Marathi: महाराष्ट्र) in Western India is the third-largest state of India by size and the second-largest by population.
It stretches from the west coast to the interior regions with a variable climate.
Maharashtra contains India's most industrialised region, the Mumbai-Pune belt.
Agriculturally, too, the state is one of India's more advanced and well-irrigated.
